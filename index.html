About CR Paradise

Located in the heart of North Carolina, CR Paradise transports you to the magical experience of Costa Rica. Our resort combines the comfort of RV camping with the warmth and "Pura Vida" spirit of Costa Rica. Enjoy our tropical trails, exotic wildlife, and relaxed atmosphere that will make you feel like you're in this Central American paradise.
